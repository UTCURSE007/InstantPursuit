THE MISSION

Every year, millions face life-altering decisions with terrible information.

Coaching centres sell dreams. Relatives offer two-decade-old advice. Google is cluttered with SEO spam. Career counsellors charge ₹15,000 for a personality test and a generic PDF.

We built a different thing: an interactive map of every major career path from Class 6 onwards — with honest pros and cons, real salary ranges, and the kind of insider tips your cousin who actually went through JEE or NEET would give you over chai.
