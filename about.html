About Instant Pursuit

Free, honest career guidance for Indian students.

🎯 Our Mission

Every year, millions of Indian students face life-altering career decisions with limited access to quality guidance. Coaching centres sell dreams, relatives offer outdated advice, and Google results are cluttered with SEO spam. Instant Pursuit exists to change this.

We provide a free, interactive career exploration platform that maps out every major career path available in India — from Class 6 through working professional — with honest pros and cons, salary data, top colleges, and insider tips. No ads. No paywalls. No data collection.

📊 What We Cover

Education Stages

  • Classes 6–10 (Foundation)
  • Classes 11–12 (Specialization)
  • Graduation
  • Post-Graduation
  • Working Professionals

Career Streams

  • Science (Engineering, Medicine, Research)
  • Commerce (CA, MBA, Finance, Banking)
  • Arts (Law, Design, Media, Psychology)
  • Vocational (ITI, Hotel Management)
  • Alternative Paths (Freelancing, Abroad)

For Each Path

  • Detailed description
  • Benefits & drawbacks
  • Top colleges
  • Top recruiters
  • Average CTC / salary
  • Insider tips
